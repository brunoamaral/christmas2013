About

Every year around Christmas Bruno comes up with something new to do. It's his way of putting up a star, of sharing with others. This year, for the first time, he asked his friend Mario for help.

Instead of the usual idea for a Photowalk or a new shiny yet boring christmas card, they went for Video. With little time and almost no margin for error, Bruno strapped a GoPro camera to his bike and cycled around the city looking to show everyone how the city is like during this time of the year.

Mario took all the footage from that one trip and took care of editing it and try to give you a little perspective on it all.

We hope you like it, and, Have a Merry Christmas!
